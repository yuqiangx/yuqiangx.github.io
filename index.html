Profile

I am a Ph.D. student in the Eyas Natural Language Processing Group at the IIE, CAS and UCAS. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yue Hu. Prior to IIE, CAS, I received B.E. in SCST from CUMT.

My academic interests are interdisciplinary, including (1) using computational methods to investigate fundamental cognitive psychological questions and (2) applying findings from cognitive psychology to develop techniques for automatic natural language processing. My long-term goal is to build AI systems that can understand, reason and create about the human mental states of participants in textual narratives, conversations, etc.
